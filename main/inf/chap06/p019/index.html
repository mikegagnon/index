Chapter 6

Supplication

Alone in the simulation room, Dr. Quimby bowed down be-
fore an imaginary camera in front of her.

“We supplicate you... Please do not annihilate us...” she said.
She heard an infinite echo of herself over the speakers of the

Infinite Corridor: “We supplicate you... Please do not anni-
hilate us...”
